Personality

Selen has an energetic, trickster-type personality. She loves to laugh and to make other people laugh. Selen enjoys messing with people, and is more likely to taunt others rather than brag about herself.

She is especially well-known for her high skill level in Apex Legends and Overwatch. She has a strong love for gaming in general, especially shooters and retro games.

While she often comes off as loud and chaotic, Selen also has a cute, wholesome side that becomes especially noticeable when drawing or making bentos, and has gained a reputation for "gap moe." She's very hardworking and often goes to great lengths to support her fellow EN Livers.
